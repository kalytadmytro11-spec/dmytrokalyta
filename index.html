01 — About

A designer who codes, an engineer who cares about pixels.

Portrait · 1280×1600

I'm a multidisciplinary product designer based in Lisbon, originally from the Pacific Northwest. For the last eight years I've helped teams ship interfaces that feel obvious in hindsight — the kind users barely notice because nothing gets in their way.

My background sits between design and engineering: I prototype in code, run design systems end-to-end, and care about animation curves and bundle sizes in equal measure. Outside of work I'm probably climbing, learning Portuguese (badly), or fixing my analog camera.
